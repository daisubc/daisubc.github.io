We are organizing a full-day pre-congress workshop at the IFAC World Congress 2026 in Busan, Republic of Korea on **Sunday, August 23, 2026**.

**LLMs and Agents for Process Industries: Foundations, Applications, and Open Challenges**

Co-organized by Prof. Bhushan Gopaluni (UBC), Prof. Mehmet Mercangöz (Imperial College London), and Prof. Chunhui Zhao (Zhejiang University). The workshop covers foundations of LLM agents, hands-on agent building, applications in process design / autonomous control / simulation, and a structured discussion of open problems.

See the workshop page for the full schedule, prerequisites, and registration details: [/ifac2026/]({{ site.baseurl }}/ifac2026/).
